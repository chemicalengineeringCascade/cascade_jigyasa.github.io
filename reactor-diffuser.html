Reactor Diffuser - Problem-Solving Competition

For Registration

Event Overview

Reactor Diffuser is a multi-round problem-solving competition designed to test the engineering minds of participants. Think fast, solve smart, and prove your technical prowess.

Date: 10th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Venunarayanan – 8807265294
  • Devamugunthaa – 7806844717 / 9443717531
  • Tharani Ponnusamy – 8300468171

Registration

  • Participants can compete individually or in a team of up to 2 members.
  • Participants must register before the event.

Event Format

  • Round 1: MCQ Round – Technical and aptitude MCQs to qualify as problem solvers.
  • Round 2: Team vs. Team Challenge – Head-to-head problem-solving to eliminate and advance.
  • Round 3: Reactor Diffuser Core Round – Solve a set of 5–6 advanced problems as fast and accurately as possible.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • MCQ Accuracy in Round 1
  • Competitive performance in Round 2
  • Speed and precision in the final round

Fair Play

  • Plagiarism, misrepresentation, or unethical behavior leads to disqualification.

Winner Selection

  • Final winners are those who solve the most problems accurately in the shortest time in Round 3.
  • Judges’ decision is final.

Prizes

  • Certificates of merit and either cash prizes or books will be awarded to the top performers.
