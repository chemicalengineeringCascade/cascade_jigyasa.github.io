Off Script - Extempore Competition

For Registration

Event Overview

Off Script is an extempore speech competition designed to challenge your ability to speak on the spot with minimal or no preparation. Topics will be related to Chemical Engineering.

Date: 10th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Arthi – 7810075589
  • Thamizharsan – 6385237125

Event Format

  • Individual participation only.
  • Topic will be given on the spot.
  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes.
  • Speech Time Limit: Up to 5 minutes.

Pre-Event Guidelines

  • Register at least one day before the event.
  • Participants must arrive 15 minutes before the start time.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • Content, originality, clarity, and focus.
  • Use of examples, facts, and reasoning.

Fair Play

  • Any misbehavior or rule violation will lead to disqualification.

Winner Selection

  • Judges will evaluate and decide based on performance and scores.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates and either cash prizes or books.
  • All participants will receive certificates.
