Mock Shark Tank - Startup Idea Pitch

For Registration

Event Overview

Mock Shark Tank is a business idea pitch competition where participants showcase their startup concepts in front of a panel of judges. Ideas will be evaluated based on originality, feasibility, business strategy, and pitch quality.

Date: 11th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Anarkali – 902569422
  • Devamugunthaa – 7806844717 / 9443717531

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants can compete individually or in a team of up to 2 members.
  • Participants must register before the event.
  • Each team has 5 minutes to pitch, followed by up to 3 minutes Q & A.
  • Presenting a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) or visual aid is optional.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation & Originality
  • Feasibility & Market Potential
  • Presentation & Communication
  • Business Model & Strategy
  • Q & A Handling

Judges' Investment Mechanism

  • Judges will allocate a fake investment amount secretly based on performance.
  • The highest invested team will be declared the winner.
  • Investment amounts will be revealed only during prize distribution.

Fair Play

  • Misrepresentation, plagiarism, or unethical behavior will lead to disqualification.

Registration

  • Register online or on-site before the event.
  • Teams can have a maximum of 2 members.

Winner Selection

  • Judges will calculate final scores and investments.
  • Top performers will be awarded based on total investment and score.

Prizes

  • Winners receive certificates and either cash prizes or technical books.
