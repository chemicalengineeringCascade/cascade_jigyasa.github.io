Floor Cross - For & Against Debate

For Registration

Event Overview

Floor Cross is a debate competition where participants must argue both for and against a given topic. This event tests your reasoning, speaking, and ability to see both sides of an issue.

Date: 11th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Anarkali – 9025669422
  • Tharani Ponnusamy – 8300468171

Registration

  • Participants must register individually. Only single participants are allowed.
  • Participants must register before the event.

Event Format

  • Participants will be given a topic prior to the debate.
  • Each participant must argue both FOR and AGAINST the given topic.
  • Time limit: 3 minutes per side (Total: 6 minutes per participant).
  • The order of speaking will be determined by the coordinators.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • Content & Relevance
  • Clarity & Delivery
  • Counterpoints & Justification
  • Time Management
  • Overall Impact

Fair Play

  • Any form of misbehavior, disrespect, or violation of event rules will lead to disqualification.

Winner Selection

  • The winner will be chosen based on the judges' decision after evaluating scores.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates of merit and either cash prizes or books.
