Event Overview

Chem Pictionary is a fun and engaging image-based chemical clue-solving game. Participants will battle through three thrilling rounds of increasing difficulty designed to test your chemistry knowledge, logic, and speed.

Date: 10th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Venunarayanan – 8807265294
  • Guru Saran – 9123565934

Rules and Format

  • Participation: Solo or in teams of 2.
  • Round 1: Image Identification – 2 images (5 points each)
  • Round 2: Image Connection – 3 images (7 points each)
  • Round 3: Rapid Fire Buzzer – Multiple images (10 points each)
  • Judgment is based on accuracy, reasoning, and timing.

Fair Play & Winner Selection

  • Strictly no misconduct or cheating — leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Winners are those with the highest cumulative score.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates and either cash prizes or books.
