Brain Teaser - Memory Game

For Registration

Event Overview

Brain Teaser is a thrilling memory game that challenges participants to recall technical images related to chemical engineering. The event consists of three increasingly difficult rounds focused on speed, accuracy, and memory retention.

Date: 11th April 2025

Registration

  • Individual participation only.
  • Participants must register before the event.

Game Format

The game involves showing a set of images from core chemical engineering topics. Participants must recall and write down the correct names based on memory alone.

Round 1

  • 30 images in 1 minute (1 image every 2 seconds)
  • Participants write down names based on memory.
  • Speed and accuracy matter.

Round 2

  • 40 images in 1 minute (1 image every 1.5 seconds)
  • Increased difficulty and pace.

Round 3

  • 25 images in 1 minute (1 image every 2.4 seconds)
  • Exact sequence required, with negative marking for errors.

General Rules

  • Answers must be submitted within the round time.
  • Points awarded based on speed and accuracy.
  • Negative marking in Round 3.
  • Use of mobile phones or cheating will lead to disqualification.

Winner Selection

  • Judges will evaluate based on performance across all rounds.
  • Top scorers will be declared winners.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates of merit and cash prizes.
