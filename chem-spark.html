CHEM SPARK SUMMIT - Technical/Poster Presentation

For Registration

Event Overview

CHEM SPARK SUMMIT is a paper presentation event where participants present their work on chemical or allied engineering topics. Presentations are judged on clarity, delivery, content depth, and creativity.

Date: 11th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • K. Susmitha – 9944962654
  • S. Lingeswari – 7010341375

Event Format

  • Group participation of 2-3 members.
  • Topic must be related to Chemical and allied Engineering.

General Rules

  • 10 minutes for presentation, followed by up to 5 minutes of Q& A.
  • Judges' decision based on content, communication skills, and feasibility.

Pre-event Guidelines

  • Register at least one day prior to the event.
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for attendance and seating.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • Content clarity and coherence
  • Organization and delivery
  • Depth of research
  • Use of examples and visual aids

Fair Play

  • Any form of misbehavior or rule violation will lead to disqualification.

Winner Selection

  • Winners will be selected based on judges' scores and overall impact.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive cash prizes or technical books.
  • All participants will receive certificates.
