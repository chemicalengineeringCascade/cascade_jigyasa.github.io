Weavers - Design Contest

For Registration

Event Overview

Weavers is a design contest where participants solve real-world problems involving chemical processes, plant design, or equipment. It encourages innovation, feasibility, and practical impact.

Date: 10th April 2025

Event Coordinators

  • Subashri – 9003738324
  • Sindhujhaa – 7094601397

Registration

  • Individual participation or in a team of up to 2 members.
  • Participants must register before the event.

Event Format

  • Participants are required to address a problem involving chemical design or equipment.
  • 10 minutes will be given to explain the design.

General Rules

  • Participants must address the problem statement clearly.
  • Clarity of calculations and technical analysis is essential.
  • Originality, feasibility, and potential impact are important criteria.
  • Any form of misrepresentation, plagiarism, or unethical behaviour will lead to disqualification.

Scoring & Evaluation Criteria

  • Clarity and comprehensiveness of addressing the problem.
  • Correctness and transparency in calculations and analysis.
  • Novelty, impact, and feasibility of the design.

Winner Selection

  • Judges will evaluate based on the overall score.
  • Top scoring designs will be awarded.

Prizes

  • Winners will receive certificates of merit and cash prizes or technical books.
